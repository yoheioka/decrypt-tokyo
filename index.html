DECRYPT TOKYOとは

Decrypt Tokyoは２日間のハッカソンイベントです。東京で開催し、100名程度のブロックチェーンをこれから学びたい学生や社会人の参加者の募集を見込んでいます。本イベントを通じて、ブロックチェーン技術・アプリ・サービスの実用化を促進、ブロックチェーンのコミュニティを広げることを目指しています。

よくある質問

1. What is Decrypt Tokyo?

Decrypt Tokyo is a 2-day Blockchain Hackathon event which is going to be held in Tokyo. We will bring together 100-150 participants who are passionate about learning Blockchain technologies. By hosting Decrypt Tokyo, we would like to promote the commercialisation of Blockchain technologies, applications, and services.

2. How much does it cost to attend Decrypt Tokyo?

This event is completely free.

3. What are the topics?

The topic will be announced at the hackathon.

4. Do you provide meals?

Yes. Catering will be provided during the hackathon.

5. Do you provide meals?

Yes. Catering will be provided during the hackathon.

6. Who can participate?

Anyone from anywhere in the world can apply. Applications are based on interest in Blockchain and proven ability to write software.

7. How many people can be on a team?

You can form teams of up to 4 people. There are no restrictions for team members, so you can team up with hackers of any country or experience level!

8. I am a beginner. Am i welcome?

Yes! This is an event to learn and expand your network. Some people will be extremely talented, some just beginners, that's why it will be amazing. We’ll have talks, mentors and workshops to help you with your project; hackathons can be a great place to learn new skills in a short amount of time.

9. How can I get there?

Travel is up to you to figure out.
The event site is pretty accessible after all:
4 minutes away from Toe Oedo Line, Roppongi Station (Exit 6)
3 minutes away from Nanboku Line, Roppongi-itchome Station (Exit 1)
1 minutes away from Roppongi Yonchome Bus Stop

10. Can I compete with an existing product?

In the interest of fairness, attendees should not be working on their projects beforehand and we do not allow participants to work on pre-existing projects. Everything needs to be built from scratch during the weekend. However, you can familiarise yourself with all the tools and technologies you intend to use beforehand!

11. Who owns the ip/copyright/product?

You! By default, each coder owns her lines of code, that's copyright. The purpose of open hackathons is to spur innovation, usually with the goal of creating a technology ecosystem. We will encourage you to put your work in GPL, MIT, CC or other open source licenses so that others can continue on your progress.

12. Which Blochchains can I use? Only Bitcoin?

Any blockchain you want. Not restricted to Bitcoin. However, you need to make sure the one you is free of security risk.

13. Which programming language shall I use?

Any programming language that you are comfortable with!

14. What kind of workshops, talks, and activities will there be?

Talks will be focused on blockchain technology and application, such us solidity, scalability etc. Schedule to be announced soon.

15. How does judging work?

We will evaluate hacks based on originality, technical difficulty, usability, and impact. The details will be announced in the hackathon.

16. What should I bring?

Your laptop! (I have seen people come empty-handed to hackathons before...) And anything that can be your source of creativities.

17. Can I post the information about this hackathon on my social media account?

Of course! And include our hashtag - #DecryptTokyo when you are doing so! Meanwhile please notice that you should use the information of this hackathon for your own commercial purpose.

18. I want to be an observer, not participate.

We are afraid that only the participant are invited.

19. I have a question that is not listed here. Who should I ask?

Feel free to email us at contact@decrypt.tokyo
